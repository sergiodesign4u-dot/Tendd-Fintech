Concept July 2026 Phase 6 Petrol & Paper

The Tendd language, on one stand

The chosen direction is Petrol & Paper: precise, trustworthy, unfussy. A calm light canvas, one deep petrol accent spent only where it earns attention, and real brand logos so a charge is recognized, not decoded. Every token below carries the attribute it traces to. The base mechanism is Monzo, minus its coral; the clarity row is Apple Wallet; the category chip is Copilot, on light.

Why this one. For a user whose activation gate is trust, the direction that leads with A2, trust shown not claimed, is the most on-thesis. Deep petrol reads institutional and calm, never alarm. The two other directions, Ink & Marigold and Plum & Fog, stay on record in concept.md and in Directions.

Logos

The content layer is real brand logos, inline SVG, not stock photos. This is Mechanism 3, the transaction clarity layer: a real logo plus a real name answers "what am I paying for?" directly, so a charge is recognized (A4), not decoded. Where a brand mark is unavailable, a clean monogram tile in the same style stands in, never a grey placeholder.

Pe

Brand set to ship (Step 5), inline SVG: Netflix, Disney+, Amazon Prime, Hulu, Adobe Creative Cloud, ChatGPT, iCloud+, Notion, Spotify, Apple Music, Peloton, Strava, The New York Times, The Economist. The monogram tile (for example "Pe" for Peloton) is the fallback when no brand mark exists, in the same petrol-on-surface style.

Icons

Solar, linear weight, inline SVG (not a CDN script), one consistent 24px rounded stroke. Coverage: the four tab-bar tabs, the trust shield, and the small utility set for metadata, actions, and states.

Components

Three live components carry most of the product. Each is shown in the language, with the states a product needs. The same subscription card here is the one on Home and inside Subscription Detail, so the same card reads as the same product across screens.

1. Primary action

Petrol fill, white text, 10px radius. The accent is spent here. Secondary is a quiet ghost. Disabled drops to a desaturated petrol, still clearly the same control.

2. Subscription card (the row)

The most reused component: real logo, real name, amount, next date, a soft category chip (A4), and a quiet status tag. Active is neutral gray; a trial is a calm petrol tint, never red.
