Step 2

Global chrome

GC1 App Header and GC2 Global Tab Bar frame every steady-state screen. GC3 to GC7 are reusable Sections specified in their cluster pages, not here.

Decisions

Recommendations

  • Four tabs, no more, and no red badge counts; a quiet new-dot only.
  • The whole onboarding chain hides both header actions and the tab bar.
  • The header carries at most one contextual action.
  • On desktop the bottom tab bar becomes a left rail; the header folds into its top.
  • Tendd has no footer: it is an app, not a marketing site.

Cross-cutting

SEO, Accessibility, Mobile

Mobile. The bottom-bar-to-left-rail change is the product's main breakpoint delta and cascades to every steady-state screen.
