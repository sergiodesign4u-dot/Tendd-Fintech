Trusted with your money

Trust is earned, not claimed. Here is exactly what Tendd can and cannot do.

  • Read-only, always Tendd can see your recurring charges but can never move, spend, or touch your money.
  • Bank-level connection We connect through Plaid, the same secure service used by many major finance apps. We never see your bank password.
  • You are in control Disconnect any account or delete all of your data at any time, in one tap. It is gone when you say so.
  • We never sell your data Your financial life is yours. Tendd does not sell or share it, full stop.
