Step 1

Account and Trust

Manage sources, control data, hold the plan. Trust is ongoing, so this is the always-findable home for "what does Tendd know and how do I change or remove it". Owns GC6.

FLAG 2 resolved. Connections re-enters onboarding's Connect Bank and Add Subscription through one "Add a source" action that opens a chooser (Connect a bank / Add manually), reusing screens 1.3 and 1.4 in-app. No duplicate screens.
Decisions

Recommendations

  • Data deletion is one labeled action, two taps from You, single confirm, deletes everything.
  • The data-access explanation uses the name-the-fear pattern (M2): state the fear, deny it with specifics.
  • Free bank connections are uncapped (product decision, July 2026); Pro is depth, not connection count.

SEO, Accessibility, Mobile
