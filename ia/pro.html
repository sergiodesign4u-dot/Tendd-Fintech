Depth / Pro

History and Trends, and the Upgrade screen that carries the paid tier.

Job: History serves J-MAIN over time. Upgrade is decision-justified (D3, D4), the one screen tracing to a decision not a job.

Type: Pages, Section (GC7), States

Step 1

Depth / Pro

The two surfaces that carry the paid tier: History and Trends, and Upgrade. Owns GC7.

Decisions

Recommendations

  • Upgrade is only ever reached from a real gate and always shows which gate the person came from; never a nav item.
  • Free is gated by depth and history, never by subscription count (D3, over the old CLAUDE.md "up to 10" line).
  • History shows a real preview frame behind the lock, not a fake blur.
  • Pricing is yearly-first with the monthly equivalent (D4).

SEO, Accessibility, Mobile
