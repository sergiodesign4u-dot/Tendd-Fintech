Step 1

Cut and Celebrate

The arc from deciding to cancel, to doing it, to feeling the win and optionally sharing it. The emotional peak and the upgrade trigger.

Decisions

Recommendations

  • Basic cancel instruction is always free and shown first; Pro depth is an addition below, never a wall (D3).
  • "Mark as cancelled" is an honest self-report at MVP; the button reads "I cancelled it".
  • A blocked cancellation has an explicit in-app next step ("Couldn't cancel?"), never a dead end.
  • Share Snapshot never contains bank data by construction (S1, E3).
  • The win uses specific monthly and yearly numbers (E2).

Cross-cutting

SEO, Accessibility, Mobile

SEO. Private and transactional. noindex. The shared card image may be posted by the person, but the screen is not indexable.

Accessibility. Steps are an ordered list; the help dialog traps focus, Escape maps to "Remind me later"; the win announces its number via aria-live; no color-only win signal.

Mobile. Desktop delta: Cancel Guide and Share Snapshot may be modals; the Win Moment stays full-attention on both.
