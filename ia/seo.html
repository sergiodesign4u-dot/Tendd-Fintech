Step 5

SEO engine

The search strategy for Tendd's one public surface, the Welcome screen. Deliberately small, because Tendd is mostly an authenticated app.

Scope. Public and indexable today: Welcome (1.1) only. Everything from Path Choice (1.2) and Guided Reveal (1.5) onward is private. A future public marketing site, outside this app repo, could reuse this engine.
Decisions

Writing principles

  • Calm over clever. No "STOP wasting money" fear copy; the promise is relief.
  • Trust up front. Lead with "no bank connection needed to start".
  • Name the person, not the power user. Avoid budget, net worth, spreadsheet.
  • Own the uncontested differentiator and privacy terms first, compete on commercial intent second, head terms are the long game.
